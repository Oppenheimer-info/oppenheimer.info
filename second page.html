ABOUT JULIAN ROBERT OPPENHEIMER

WHO WAS OPPENHEIMER?

Born: 22nd April 1904, new york, princeton, new jersey, united states

Oppenheimer is well-known as a top physicist and leader of the Manhattan Project, which developed the atomic bomb during World War II.

He is often called the "father" of the atomic bomb due to his significant role in its creation and deployment by the United States.

children: Peter Oppenheimer and Katherine Oppenheimer

Spouse: Katherine Oppenheimer Vissering

Died on: FEBRUARY 18 1967 in Princeton, New Jersey
